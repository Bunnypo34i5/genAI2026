## ‚úçÔ∏è Step 3: Draft Your Essay (20 minutes)

### Suggested Structure (200 words)

**Opening (30-40 words)**
- Briefly introduce what you're reflecting on
- Mention both regression and simulation modeling
- Preview your main insight or learning

**Main Body (120-150 words)**
- **Paragraph 1:** Discuss your learning with regression and simulation
  - What you learned about each approach
  - How they differ or complement each other
  - Specific examples from class or exercises
  
- **Paragraph 2:** Connect to your group project
  - Which technique(s) could apply to your project
  - How these models help you think about your project differently
  - Potential applications or use cases

- **Paragraph 3:** Personal insights and growth
  - Challenges you faced and overcame
  - Key realizations or "aha!" moments
  - How your understanding has evolved

**Conclusion (30-40 words)**
- Summarize your key learning
- Look forward to applying these skills
- Express how this knowledge shapes your approach to data analysis

### Writing Tips:

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Use first person ("I learned...", "I noticed...")
- Be specific and genuine
- Include concrete examples
- Show critical thinking
- Connect ideas logically
- Use clear, professional language

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Just summarize course content
- Be vague or generic
- List facts without reflection
- Skip the project connection
- Rush without proofreading

### Sample Opening Sentences:

- "Exploring regression and simulation modeling in GCAP 3226 has fundamentally changed how I approach data analysis..."
- "Initially, I viewed regression and simulation as similar techniques, but through our coursework I've discovered..."
- "The most significant insight from learning regression and simulation modeling has been understanding when to apply each approach..."
